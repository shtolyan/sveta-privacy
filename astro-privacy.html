Privacy Policy

App: Aether Veil  ·  Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IE Anatolii Sheshenin ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Aether Veil mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide astrological calculations:

  • Location (approximate) — used to calculate sunrise/sunset times, planetary hours, and other location-based astrological data. Location is processed on-device and is never stored on our servers.
  • Date of birth — entered voluntarily by the user for natal chart calculations. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase information — processed by Apple App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To perform astrological calculations (planetary positions, lunar phases, planetary hours)
  • To personalize your experience (natal chart, biorhythms, destiny matrix)
  • To manage your subscription status

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Expo / EAS — app infrastructure (expo.dev/privacy)

4. Data Storage

All personal data (birth date, location preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Aether Veil is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Revoke location permission in your device settings
  • Request information about data we hold by contacting us

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
