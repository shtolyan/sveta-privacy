Privacy Policy

App: Kura Money  ·  Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IE Anatolii Sheshenin ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Kura Money mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Kura Money does not require registration, does not track your activity, and does not send any data to external servers. All information you enter in the app is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

2. Data Storage

All data created within the app (notes, settings, and other user content) is stored:

  • Locally on your device — using the device's built-in storage.
  • In your iCloud account — for synchronization between your Apple devices. This data is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We have no access to your iCloud data. We do not operate any servers that store your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit) — for data synchronization across your devices, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • TelemetryDeck — a privacy-friendly analytics service. We use it to understand which sections of the app are used most often so we can improve the experience. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data, does not track individual users, and does not use advertising identifiers. All signals are anonymous and aggregated. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising or user-tracking services.

4. Children's Privacy

Kura Money does not collect personal information from any user, including children. The app is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account, you have full control over it. You may at any time:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application.
  • Manage iCloud data in your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
